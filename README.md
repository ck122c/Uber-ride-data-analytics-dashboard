# Uber-ride-data-analytics-dashboard

🚖 Uber Ride Data Analytics Dashboard
📊 Project Overview
This project presents a comprehensive end-to-end data analytics solution built using Excel, SQL, and Power BI to analyze Uber ride operations and customer behavior.
The objective was to transform raw ride-level transactional data into meaningful business insights that support operational and strategic decision-making.
The final solution is a 5-page interactive dashboard that tracks bookings, revenue performance, customer patterns, vehicle utilization, cancellations, and geographic activity.

🎯 Business Objectives
Monitor ride demand and booking trends
Identify revenue contribution by vehicle category
Analyze ride cancellations and reasons
Understand customer behavior and retention
Track operational performance across time
Improve decision-making using interactive analytics

🧰 Tools & Technologies Used
Tool	Purpose
Excel	Initial data cleaning & formatting
SQL	Data querying, aggregation & transformation
Power BI	Data modeling & dashboard visualization

🔄 Data Preparation & Processing

Step 1 — Data Cleaning (Excel)
Removed null and duplicate records
Standardized date & time formats
Categorized vehicle types
Corrected inconsistent values
Prepared structured dataset

Step 2 — Data Transformation (SQL)
Performed analytical queries:
Total bookings & completed rides
Revenue calculations
Cancellation segmentation
Customer frequency analysis
Vehicle performance metrics

Step 3 — Data Modeling (Power BI)
Created star schema model
Built Calendar table for time intelligence
Created relationships
Designed calculated columns & measures

Revenue

📊 Dashboard Pages & Insights

1️⃣ Overview Page
Displays high-level operational KPIs:
Completed bookings
Lost bookings
Revenue generated
Total & average trip distance
Monthly booking trends
Revenue by vehicle type
Driver ratings & payment distribution
👉 Provides quick operational health check

2️⃣ Vehicle Analysis Page
Analyzes performance by vehicle category:
Booking volume per vehicle
Revenue contribution
Utilization comparison
Performance ranking
👉 Identifies most profitable vehicle segment

3️⃣ Revenue Analysis Page
Focuses on earnings behavior:
Monthly & quarterly revenue trends
Revenue vs customers comparison
Payment method contribution
👉 Helps business forecasting & pricing strategy

4️⃣ Rider Analysis Page
Customer behavior analytics:
New vs returning customers
Customer-wise revenue
Cancellation reasons
Ride patterns
👉 Helps improve retention & customer experience

5️⃣ Location & Operations Page
Operational performance tracking:
Busy time slots
High demand locations
Distance analysis
👉 Helps optimize supply allocation

📈 Key Insights Discovered
Auto & Bike categories generate highest ride demand
Peak bookings occur during mid-month periods
UPI is the dominant payment method
Customer cancellations form a significant loss portion
Returning riders contribute majority of revenue
Some vehicle categories have high distance but low profit efficiency
